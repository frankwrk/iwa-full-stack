Purchase Casks

Purchasing your casks is very straightforward. Simply complete the short application form, upload your photo identification and select your preferred Cask Bundle. We will then be in touch to arrange a secure bank transfer. Following your purchase, you will receive a receipt and the step by step process will begin. All Cask Bundles include the casks, spirit, storage and insurance for five years. There are no hidden charges or fees.

If you would like to make a purchase but the Cask Bundles are not ideal, please email founder@irishwhiskeyassets.com
